# 🧙‍♂️ Tales of Eldoria 🏰
*A pixel-fantasy adventure of might, magic, and mystery...*

## 📜 Overview

**Eldoria** is a fantasy-themed RPG written in Python using Pygame.  
Choose from legendary classes like the Barbarian, Wizard, Rogue, Paladin, Necromancer
and more...

Explore mysterious realms, collect enchanted equipment, face powerful enemies, and master each hero’s unique abilities!

## 🎮 Controls

- **Navigate Menus**: 🖱️ Left Click
- **Buy Items**:  
  1. 🖱️ *Left Click* to select the item  
  2. Then click **"Buy"** button

    > ⚠️ If you don't have enough gold, the **Buy** button text changes to:  
    **`Not enough gold`** 🪙

- **Equip Item**:  
  1. 🖱️ *Left Click* to select the item  
  2. 🖱️ *Right Click* to equip it

- **Unequip Item**:  
  🖱️ *Right Click* on equipped item

- **Use Special Abilities**: Depending on the class, mana or stamina is consumed

---

## ⚔️ Features

- Unique classes with their own mana/stamina & abilities
- Inventory management & equipment system
- Tactical item usage: weapons, armor, relics, potions
- Ambient fantasy soundtrack with mute/unmute, volume-control  and UI
- Unique Companions to find for the specific classes
